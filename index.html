Health Guidance for Adults

Get Tested for HIV

Get tested for HIV at least once. You may need to get tested more often depending on your risk.

Get Your Blood Pressure Checked

Get your blood pressure checked once a year.

Get the Seasonal Flu Vaccine

Get the flu vaccine every year to protect yourself and others from the flu.

Talk with a Doctor About Your Alcohol Use

If you are concerned about your drinking, ask your doctor about screening and counseling.

Get Shots to Protect Your Health

Get important adult shots (vaccinations).
