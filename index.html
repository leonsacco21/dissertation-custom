Health Tips for Teens

Get Tested for HIV

Description: All teens this age need to get tested for HIV at least once. (USPSTF)

Get the Seasonal Flu Vaccine

Description: Your teen needs to get the flu vaccine every year. (ACIP)

Get Screened for Depression

Description: Ask your son’s doctor to screen him for depression, even if you don’t see signs of a problem. (USPSTF)

Get a Physical Exam Each Year

Description: Your son needs a checkup at least once a year. The doctor or nurse will check to make sure he’s healthy and developing on schedule. (HRSA)

Get Screened for Obesity

Description: Talk to your teen’s doctor to make sure your son is at a healthy weight. (USPSTF)
