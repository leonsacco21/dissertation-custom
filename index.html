Health Recommendations for Young Adults

Get Your Blood Pressure Checked

Get your blood pressure checked regularly - at least every 5 years.

Get Help to Quit Smoking

If you smoke, ask your doctor about services to help you quit.

Get Tested for Hepatitis C

Everyone ages 18 to 79 needs to get tested for hepatitis C.

Get Shots to Protect Your Health

Get important adult shots (vaccinations).

Talk with a Doctor About Your Alcohol Use

If you are concerned about your drinking, ask your doctor about screening and counseling.
